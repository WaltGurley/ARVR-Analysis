Textual Analysis

  • When a request length was specified most requesters indicated they would like to use the device within the span of one day, though length requests ranged from a few hours to one week.
  • The overwhelming motivation for requesters to use equipment was for research or development
  • Requests for the Oculus Rift were related more to 3D and game design while requests for Google Glass were for more utilitarian purposes
  • Computer science and engineering were the most represented identifiable fields of study for requesters.
